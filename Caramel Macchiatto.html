Caramel Macchiatto

150 PHP


A Caramel Macchiato is made by mixing vanilla syrup and steamed milk together, and then pouring the espresso on top, leaving the black mark on the milk foam ("macchiato" means "marked" in Italian). After, caramel sauce is drizzled on top of the drink.
